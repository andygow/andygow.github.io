How GowTech can work with you

Pieces of work

This is the best solution for many clients. We will discuss your requirements with you then propose work pieces of design and development that are delivered in phases for you to approve, with key deliverables at the end of each phase. If you are happy, then we can continue with the next phase of work.

We can work with in-house engineering resource or independently.

Fee for time

This option can be better if you have uncertain goals, or simply need more design or CAD resource in your business for a period. We simply charge an hourly rate for time spent at the company, so you can benefit from top mechanical design skill where you need it, without committing to extra employees. We can be present in-house, or work remotely and visit you regularly.
