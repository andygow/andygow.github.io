About Us

Who we are

GowTech was founded by Andy Gow, a mechanical design engineer with experience in Consumer, Industrial and Medical product design for clients all over the world. We are based in Wellington, New Zealand.

What we can do for you

We offer mechanical product design services for consumer, medical, industrial and scientific markets, and specialise in the hard projects and integration with electronics and firmware. We can develop your idea from a sketch right through to a finished product being delivered to your warehouse. Our deliverable to you is not limited to:

  • Requirements specification documentation
  • Concept sketches and CAD
  • Detailed design CAD
  • 3D printed or machined functional prototypes with working elements
  • Representative visual prototypes with appropriate finish, logos, and feel
  • Process design
  • Finished, working machines

Some of our specialist skills

  • FEA - Computer analysis of forces, stresses, strains to see if your product is strong enough and how it can be improved, strength-for-weight.
  • Driveworks - creating an automated Solidworks based platform for the creation of works orders, detailed drawings, and customer quotes.
  • Gearing - modelling of gear-trains incorporating spur, helical, bevel, racks, ratchets, pawls, and worm drives with performance modelling and motor matching.
  • Beverage machine design for food safety - coffee, tea, cold liquids
  • Thermal and vibration analysis
  • Robotic drives incorporating motors with sensing electronics
  • Bearing and seal design for high performance in harsh environments
  • Electronics and software - integrating electronics and software elements considering interconnects, EMI, battery charging, wiring looms, heat rejection.
  • Design-For-Manufacture (DFM) - principles including functional simplification, Poka-yoke, mouldability, reliability analysis.
  • Plastic injection mould features including snap-fits, live hinges, and mechanisms.
  • Sheet metal modelling of advanced 3D designs, ready for laser cutting or waterjet.
